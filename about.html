Behind The Lens

The Visionary.

Documenting Life's Truest Moments.

My journey with a camera began over a decade ago. What started as a fascination with light and shadow quickly evolved into a lifelong obsession with human connection.

At VDS Photography, my philosophy is simple: I don't just want to show you what your day looked like. I want to show you exactly how it felt. I blend editorial fashion techniques with raw, photojournalistic storytelling to create timeless art.

Based in New York, capturing love stories worldwide.
